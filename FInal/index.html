Now & Forever

Let's Tie The Knot

On your special day, you want to focus on making memories. Our goal? To capture them perfectly on camera. Since 2000, we have been helping happy couples document their special day. Now & Forever is here to help you find the perfect balance between capturing the big moments, as well as the small and intimate ones. Let’s make magic happen together. Check out our site to see what we’ve done for other happy couples.
My name is Kendall Furrow and I've always had a passion for photography. There is something so special, so endearing about capturing those once-in-a-lifetime kind of moments. I love to seek out those intimate and joyful moments, capturing the essence of every couple’s very special day on camera. I am such a people person as I grew up in a big family along with being a part of the soccer team at the University of Montana. So I love to focus on exclusively making memories for couples and I love getting to know the people that my team and I work with! It is the added personal touch that allows us to photograph truly beautiful and unique moments that reflect your very special day.
As an experienced Wedding Photography , I have photographed a wide variety of events and have a team of highly trained professionals. The perfect day begins with you; your originality, taste, and style inspire everything we do. My parents showed me what the perfect love story looks like which is what inspires me to help you illustrate your love story through our lenses, providing you with a collection of images that look and feel as authentic as your love.
